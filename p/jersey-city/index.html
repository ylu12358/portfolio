Side quest with the nocticron

Apr 19, 2024

I was hoping the Leica 12-60 would be usable for indoor sports photography, but alas, I proved myself wrong. Shots at 1/1000 came out quite grainy, and f/2.8 just doesn’t cut it indoors. I also have the Lumix 25mm, but it’s slightly too tight to shoot comfortably. I often find myself having to sit in the middle of the next court, which is a no-go at busy tournaments. I considered the Lumix 20mm, but I ended up deciding against it due to its reputation for lackluster AF. The next logical step was the Leica 15mm.
